Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any bike listing details (including photos and descriptions).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To facilitate the buying and selling of bicycles.
  • To verify your identity via Google, Email, or OTP.
  • To improve our AI-powered bike detection and search features.

3. Singapore PDPA Compliance

In accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) of Singapore, we do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your phone number is only used for OTP verification and is not displayed publicly unless you choose to include it in a listing.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with BikeStop.Store to provide a faster, smoother experience.
